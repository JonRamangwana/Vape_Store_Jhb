What’s the difference between a disposable & rechargeable vape?


Post last update: 26 April 2023
Vapes have different shapes & sizes. Availability of all these various vape products can make selection of one product difficult. One notable difference among vape products is whether they are disposable or not. We are going to delve into these differences to provide a better understanding of vapes.

Source: Pixabay

To waste no time here is a list of differences between disposable & rechargeable vapes:

1. Refill
Disposable vapes are sold with e-juice that cannot get replaced. On the other hand rechargeable vapes are also known as refillable vapes meaning they can get refilled. Rechargeable vapes have a tank that can be filled with juice when device runs out. Juice can be bought in a big container to fill the small pods that are placed inside the vape.

2. Recharge
All rechargeable vapes can get charged when battery runs low. Disposable vapes have a built in battery that cannot be removed or recharged. When battery gets empty before juice runs out there is no choice, just throw away.

Source: Pixabay

3. Dispose
Rechargeable vapes can be kept for a long time. Recharge, refill, vape away. The counterpart is less committed. Like the name says, “Disposable”. These vapes are disposed of once done with. Disposable vapes are a one night stand of the vape world.

4. Battery
Disposable vapes have a less powerful none rechargeable battery while rechargeable vapes have a high capacity battery that can be charged multiple times.

5. Maintenance
Similar to a personal hand held communication device i.e. cellphone, personal hand held vaping devices i.e. rechargeable vapes require maintenance such as cleaning, battery changes, refilling of juice and so on.

WARNING: DO NOT FILL PHONE WITH JUICE, CIRCUIT MIGHT BURN. Cellphones can only be filled with data. Unlike rechargeable vapes, disposable vapes don’t need a lot of maintenance.

6. Button
Most rechargeable vapes have a power control feature such as a button. While most disposable vapes consist of no such feature.
